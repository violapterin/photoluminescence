@@the message of the film is, to me, <whether sinners could find happiness>; whether modern thinking {Shun $advocates in the club} is compatible with the historic building, and whether Umi and Shun from the younger generation may take delight in their romance, in the aftermath of the war declared by the older.@@ _
@@Japan @lost the world war, and @wanted to prove its reform in the 1964 Olympic;@@
@@it @underwent the financial crisis, and @sought to show its power in the 2021 Olympic.@@ _
@@this is what Japan, a rather odd country, has been dealing with for decades.@@ _
